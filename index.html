simpel omelette recipe

an easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.this classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice,vegetables,or meats

perparation time

  • total:approximately10 minuts
      preparation:5 minuts
      cooking:5 minuts

    • ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • peppare,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

    • Instructions

  • 1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can ade a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  • 2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butte; or oil.
  • 3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs:
  • 4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  • 5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onfoa plate.
  • 6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
